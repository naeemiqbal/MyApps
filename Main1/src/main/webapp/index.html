About Naeem



This is Naeem Iqbal, a passionate Java developer in Greenville SC. Working at Infor, but started at Datastream Systems Inc in April 1999.

Born in Pakistan and raised in Nigeria and Pakistan, I graduated from Foundation of Science & Technology (FAST) Institute of Computer Science Lahore [then affiliated with University of Punjab] now part of National University Computer and Emerging Sciences of in December 1992.

I came to the United States in July 1998. Lived in Kansas City MO for 7 months before coming to Greenville.
